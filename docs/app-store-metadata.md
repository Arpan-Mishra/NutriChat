## Description

Track your calories and macros effortlessly — from your iPhone or WhatsApp.

NutriChat is a simple, focused calorie tracker that syncs with a WhatsApp bot called NutriBot. Log meals in the app or just text "logged 2 rotis for dinner" to NutriBot on WhatsApp — everything stays in sync.

KEY FEATURES

- Calorie & Macro Tracking — Log meals with detailed nutrition info (calories, protein, fat, carbs). See daily progress with a visual calorie ring and macro bars.

- Powerful Food Search — Search across 5 databases (USDA, Open Food Facts, Edamam, FatSecret, and our local database) for accurate nutrition data. Supports Indian foods, packaged products, and restaurant items.

- Barcode Scanner — Scan packaged food barcodes to instantly pull up nutrition facts.

- WhatsApp Bot Integration — Connect NutriBot to log meals, check your totals, and manage your diary right from WhatsApp. Generate an API key in the app, send it to the bot, and you're linked.

- Meal Sections — Organize entries by Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Snacks. Edit or delete any entry with a tap.

- Goals & Progress — Set personalised calorie and macro goals based on your profile. Track progress with visual indicators.

- Recently Logged Foods — Quick-add from your recent meals without searching again.

- Custom Foods — Add your own food items with custom nutrition values.

- Date Navigation — Swipe between days to review past entries.

PRIVACY FIRST
Your data stays yours. We don't sell or share your personal information. API keys are hashed, tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain, and all communication is encrypted.

Get started in under a minute — enter your phone number, set up your profile, and start logging.
