NutriChat Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 28, 2026

NutriChat ("we", "our", "the app") is a calorie-tracking iOS application developed by Arpan Mishra. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

  • Phone number — used for account creation and OTP verification. We do not store your contacts.
  • Profile information — name, date of birth, sex, height, weight, and activity level. Provided voluntarily during onboarding to calculate your calorie and macro goals.
  • Food diary entries — meals you log, including food name, serving size, calories, and macronutrients. Stored on our servers so you can access them across devices.
  • Weight logs — optional weight entries you record to track progress.
  • Camera (barcode scanning) — the app accesses your camera only when you initiate a barcode scan. Images are processed on-device and are never uploaded or stored.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Display your food diary, calorie totals, and macro progress in the app.
  • Compute personalised calorie and macro goals based on your profile.
  • Sync diary entries between the iOS app and the WhatsApp bot (NutriBot) when you choose to link them via an API key.
  • Improve the food database by caching search results from public nutrition APIs (USDA, Open Food Facts, Edamam, FatSecret).

3. WhatsApp Bot Integration

If you choose to connect NutriBot (our WhatsApp bot), you generate a personal API key inside the app. This key allows the bot to read and write diary entries on your behalf. You can revoke the key at any time from the app, which immediately disconnects the bot.

We do not read your WhatsApp messages. The bot only processes messages you send directly to it.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored on PostgreSQL databases hosted on Railway (cloud infrastructure provider) with encrypted connections.
  • Authentication tokens (JWT) are stored in the iOS Keychain, not in UserDefaults or plain storage.
  • API keys are hashed with bcrypt before storage — the raw key is shown to you exactly once and never stored in plaintext on our servers.
  • All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

5. Third-Party Services

We query the following public food databases to provide nutrition information:

  • USDA FoodData Central
  • Open Food Facts
  • Edamam Food Database API
  • FatSecret Platform API

Your search queries are sent to these services to return nutrition results. No personal information (name, phone, diary) is shared with them.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers. Your data is only used to provide the NutriChat service to you.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile tab in the app. Upon deletion, all your personal data (profile, diary entries, weight logs, API keys) is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

NutriChat is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app. The "Effective date" at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last updated.
